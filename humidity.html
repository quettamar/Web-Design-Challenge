Humidity

At first, there didn't appear to be any kind of correlation between latitude and humidity but after looking at a world map, the outliers between 0 and 40 degrees latitude could include the Saharan Desert which explains the big dips in humidity. Between -20 to -40 degrees latitude more outliers could be explained by one of the test cities being in Australia or South Africa. Other than those outliers, there is not an apparent correlation between latitude and humidity.
